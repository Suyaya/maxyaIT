Rival Shot

Description:

Automatically find and score shots on a paper target.

Role:

Lead developer and algorithm designer

Initial concept demonstration development

challenges:

Melzi SharpsFinder

Melzi SharpsFinder

Description:

Find misplaced needles during laparoscopic surgery.

Role:

Firmware and algorithm development

challenges:

Always, it’s a challenge to balance speed, timing, and power requirements for any device. Being a medical device, it was even more paramount that hardware errors are handled safely in a way that does not lead to harm for the surgeon and patient. Finally, it required an algorithm that could reliable process all the sensor data in a robust, consistent manner.

outdoor recorder

Outdoor Recorder

Description:

Low-power field deployable recording device.

Role:

Firmware and hardware designer

challenges:

Initial challenges were integrating the various hardware components (microphone, clock, power, LCD UI) in a way that allowed full power control, sleep modes, and proper timestamps. The device is intended to be deployed to the field (aquatic and on-land use) for extended durations of many months.
